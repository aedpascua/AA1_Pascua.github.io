Haikyu!!

Haikyu is an anime series all about a volleyball team and their adventurous and exciting journey to the nationals, representing their school in order to compete against other teams. Throughout the series, the team seeks their full potential; training tirelessly to achieve their destiny, and fulfill their passion for the sport.
